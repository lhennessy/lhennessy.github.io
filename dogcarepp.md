# Privacy Policy for DogCare

**Last updated: 24 May 2026**
**Developer: Liam Hennessy**

This Privacy Policy explains how DogCare ("the app", "we", "I") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the DogCare iOS application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Pet profiles** — name, date of birth, breed, sex, weight, and photos you add for your dogs
- **Care tasks** — task titles, notes, due dates, and completion history you create
- **Weight entries** — weight logs and associated notes
- **Emergency contacts** — names, phone numbers, and notes for contacts you add

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Crash reports and usage analytics** — collected via Google Firebase to help identify and fix bugs and improve the app. This data is anonymised and does not include personally identifiable information.
- **Device information** — basic device type and iOS version, collected as part of crash reporting.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and operate the app's core features (pet care tracking, reminders, weight history)
- To sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud (using CloudKit)
- To diagnose crashes and improve app stability via Firebase Crashlytics
- To understand general usage patterns and improve the app via Firebase Analytics

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## iCloud and CloudKit

Your pet and care data is stored on your device and, if you are signed into iCloud, synced via Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not have access to data stored in your iCloud account.

---

## Firebase (Google)

DogCare uses Firebase services provided by Google, including:

- **Firebase Crashlytics** — for crash reporting
- **Firebase Analytics** — for anonymised usage analytics

Data collected by Firebase is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Firebase Analytics data is anonymised; we do not use it to identify individual users.

---

## Photos and Camera

If you add a photo to a dog's profile, that image is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account. Photos are not uploaded to any server controlled by us.

---

## Notifications

DogCare may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming care tasks. These notifications are generated on-device and do not involve transmitting any data to external servers.

---

## Data Retention

Your data is stored on your device and in your iCloud account for as long as you use the app. You can delete individual entries within the app at any time. To delete all data, remove the app from your device; this will also remove locally stored data. iCloud data can be managed via your iCloud settings.

---

## Children's Privacy

DogCare is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
